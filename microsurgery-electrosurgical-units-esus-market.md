<p><p>Microsurgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are advanced surgical devices utilized for cutting and coagulating tissue using electrical currents. The market is primarily segmented into three types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single active electrode for cutting and cauterizing; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, where current flows between two electrodes for precise tissue interaction; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically to close blood vessels securely. Each type serves distinct functions, enhancing surgical efficiency and patient safety in microsurgical procedures.</p></p>
