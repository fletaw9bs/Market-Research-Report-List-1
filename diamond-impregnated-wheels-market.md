<p><strong>The Diamond-Impregnated Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sintering Type</li><li>Electroplating</li><li>Brazing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond-impregnated wheels market comprises three primary types: sintering, electroplating, and brazing. Sintering involves the bonding of diamond particles to a metallic matrix through high heat and pressure, creating durable wheels for grinding. Electroplating applies a layer of diamond onto a metal base using an electric current, resulting in precise and sharp cutting edges. Brazing uses a filler metal to bond diamonds to a substrate, offering strong adhesion and effective thermal conductivity. Each type serves specific applications in manufacturing and construction.</p></p>
