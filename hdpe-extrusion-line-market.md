<p><p>The HDPE extrusion line market is categorized into single-layer and multilayer systems. Single-layer lines produce homogenous products, ideal for applications requiring uniformity, such as pipes and films. In contrast, multilayer extrusion lines combine various layers, allowing for enhanced functionality like barrier properties or improved mechanical strength. This versatility meets diverse industry demands, such as packaging and construction. The choice between single-layer and multilayer systems depends on specific product requirements and desired material characteristics.</p></p>
